‘Section 375’ review

A movie directed by Ajay Bahl starring Richa Chadda, Akshay Khanna, Meera Chopra and Rahul Bhatis a courtroomdramawith a sensitive topic. Itnot only grabs attention till the last moment but also opens several topics for debate and presents the bitter truth of Judicial System. It is about how the law could be misused and even if the decision has been given according to the law but justice might not happen.

At the starting of the movie, Saluja (Akshay K) gives the example of Nirbhaya's case that how anaccusedminor got freeafter 3 years serving in jailsalthough he is primarily responsible for her deathas he is tried at JuvenileCourt.A costume designer Anjali (Meera C) accusesmovie director Khurana (Rahul B) of raping her. A Sessions Court sentenced to 10 years of rigorous imprisonment and the case is reopened in HighCourt. Khurana hires a high profile lawyer Saluja and in opposition to him, Hiral Gandhi, a public prosecutor gives different sub-plots to the situation. As a case progresses, it takes interesttill the end.

The excellent part of this movie are dialogues and acting. Every character of the movie, especially Akshay Khanna did their jobs perfectly and not a single stepwrong. Actor Kishore Kadam, who plays the role of Judge in High Court impresses viewers with his attitude and dialogues. This movie makes us think from the other side also to any case.The courtroom dramapresentedvery well without any overactingwhich makes the movie real.

‘Section 375’ is an audacious step that will not disappoint you and should be once watched by everyone.
