LET ME BE AS I WANT TO

Nishi Bhate

22 Feb

LET ME BE AS I WANT TO

I can get betrayed, but I cannot betray,
He can drink and get me deserted, but I cannot drink,
He can use coarse words, but if I use it’s scurrility,
He can be a carouser,
But if I’m the one
I seem to drown my dynasty.
LET ME BE AS I WANT TO.
I am discerned by the society
for the curves of my body,
for the coolness of my being,
for the pudgy of my body,
for the length of my skirt,
for being assertive at my work,
LET ME BE AS I WANT TO.

Men who sleep around multiple are studs,
but women who do the same are sluts,
Judge me not by appearance,
I came to Earth as I am.
I am barred from wearing skirts,
Because legs are bare.
Men who sing, dance are artists,
grabbing their passion.
Women who sing and dance are
Unproductive,
nugatory
and groundless.
LET ME BE AS I WANT TO.

I’m not a saint,
Neither are you
I Am not an angel,
but in gods eyes I am.
I’ve made mistakes,
so did you
I’ve made bad choices,
just like you.
My mistakes don’t determine my forthcoming.
M not perfect, same as you.
LET ME BE AS I WANT TO.

I can experiment
I can go to solo-trips
I can party late night
I can have hair on my body
I can have multiple relations
I can show my colour with any shade
LET ME BE AS I WANT TO.

Glass ceiling may be shattered,
But women still face obstacles.
May her reach to miles,
but you won’t stop pulling her down.
And I won’t allow you,
Letting me live as you desire.
I am phenomenal and I will be phenomenal
I WILL BE AS I WANT TO.
